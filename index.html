Wordle Game

how to play?

  1. Guess a 5 letter meaningful word.
  2. Click Submit to check submited word.
  3. Click Del to remove last letter.
  • Letter at right position will turn square to green.
  • Letters at wrong position will turn square to yellow.
  • Not included letters will be turn square to dark gray.
